English Clauses

  • I will call you when I arrive. - 내가 도착하면 너에게 전화할게. (naega dochakhmyeon neoege jeonhwahalge)
  • If you study hard, you will pass the exam. - 열심히 공부하면 시험에 합격할 거야. (yeolsimhi gongbuhamyeon siheome hapgyeokhal geoya)
  • He was happy because he passed the test. - 그는 시험에 합격해서 기뻤어. (geuneun siheome hapgyeokhaeseo gippeosseo)
  • When the bell rings, we will start the class. - 종이 울리면 우리는 수업을 시작할 거야. (jongi ullimyeon urineun sueobeul sijakhal geoya)
  • If I had known, I would have helped you. - 내가 알았더라면, 도와줬을 거야. (naega arasseuramyun, dowajwosseul geoya)
  • After the meeting, we went for lunch. - 회의 후 우리는 점심을 먹으러 갔어. (hoeui hu urineun jeomsimeul meogeureo gasseo)
  • Before I go to bed, I always read a book. - 나는 잠자기 전에 항상 책을 읽어. (naneun jamjagi jeone hangsang chaegeul ilgeo)
  • While he was studying, his phone rang. - 그가 공부하는 동안 그의 전화가 울렸어. (geuga gongbuhaneun dongan geuui jeonhwaga ullyeosseo)
  • We can leave as soon as the movie ends. - 영화가 끝나자마자 우리는 떠날 수 있어. (yeonghwaga kkeutnajamaja urineun tteonal su isseo)
  • Even though it was raining, they went hiking. - 비가 왔지만, 그들은 하이킹을 갔어. (biga watjiman, geudeureun haikingeul gasseo)
  • As soon as I get home, I will call you. - 집에 도착하자마자 너에게 전화할게. (jibe dochakhajamaja neoege jeonhwahalge)
  • Until she arrives, we can wait here. - 그녀가 도착할 때까지 여기서 기다릴 수 있어. (geunyeoga dochakhalkkaji yeogiseo gidaril su isseo)
  • Though he tried his best, he didn't win. - 최선을 다했지만, 그는 이기지 못했어. (choeseon-eul dahaessjiman, geuneun igiji mothaesseo)
  • She sings whenever she is happy. - 그녀는 행복할 때마다 노래해. (geunyeoneun haengbokhal ttaemada noraehae)
  • Since I moved to Seoul, I've been very busy. - 내가 서울로 이사한 이후로 매우 바빴어. (naega Seoullo isahan ihu-ro maeu babasseo)
  • As long as you work hard, you will succeed. - 열심히 일하면 성공할 거야. (yeolsimhi ilhamyeon seonggonghal geoya)
  • In case of emergency, call this number. - 비상시에 이 번호로 전화해. (bisangsi-e i beonhoro jeonhwahae)
  • Whether you like it or not, you must go. - 네가 좋아하든 말든, 너는 가야 해. (nega johahadeon maldeun, neoneun gaya hae)
  • As if he knew the answer, he raised his hand. - 마치 답을 아는 것처럼 그는 손을 들었어. (machi dab-eul aneun geotcheoreom geuneun son-eul deureosseo)
  • I will help you as much as I can. - 내가 할 수 있는 만큼 도와줄게. (naega hal su itneun mankeum dowajulge)
